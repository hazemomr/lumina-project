Our Story

Discover the passion behind Lumina and our commitment to your natural beauty and radiance.

Crafting Beauty with Passion

Founded in 2010, Lumina started as a small boutique salon with one simple mission: to provide a sanctuary of peace and luxury for every woman. We believe beauty is not just about a look, but a feeling of confidence and radiance.

Over a decade, we've evolved into a premier destination, combining organic ingredients with the latest global trends in skincare and hair styling, ensuring that every visit is a transformative experience.
